Services & Process

Six services, one accountable partner

We manage every stage between your design files and your warehouse — so a single point of contact carries the order from sketch to shipment.

SERVICE 01

Product Development & Sampling

Tech pack analysis, pattern making, and fit/proto/photo samples with fast revision turnaround before you commit to bulk.

SERVICE 02

Fabric & Trim Sourcing

Access to vetted knit and woven mills, matched by quality tier, MOQ, and price point — with lab-dip and swatch approval.

SERVICE 03

Factory & Vendor Management

Order allocation to the right partner factory based on capability, capacity, and current book — with direct on-ground coordination.

SERVICE 04

Quality Assurance & Compliance

In-line, mid-line, and final random inspections against AQL standards, plus social and technical compliance verification.

SERVICE 05

Production Monitoring

Weekly WIP and capacity reports so you always know cut, sew, and finishing status without chasing anyone for updates.

SERVICE 06

Logistics & Shipping

Export documentation, freight-forwarder coordination, and on-time delivery scheduling to your distribution center.

Our Process

Five stages, one order

A predictable path from brief to delivery — with checkpoints and reporting built in at every stage.

01
Week 0

Brief & Costing

We review your tech pack, target price, and volumes, then return a costing sheet and factory recommendation.

02
Week 1–3

Sampling & Approval

Fit and proto samples are produced and shipped for review, with revisions until sign-off.

03
Week 3–4

Vendor Allocation

Bulk fabric and trims are booked, and the order is slotted into the matched factory's production line.

04
Week 4–10

Production & QC

Cutting, sewing, and finishing proceed under in-line inspection, with weekly WIP reports sent to you.

05
Week 10+

Shipping & Delivery

Final random inspection, export documentation, and freight coordination through to your distribution center.

About MB Global Apparels

Built by merchandisers who got tired of factory guesswork

We started MB Global Apparels to close the gap between international fashion brands and Bangladesh's manufacturing floor — with honest reporting, vetted partners, and zero surprises.

Our Sourcing Office — Dhaka
Our Story

Sourcing, without the guesswork

MB Global Apparels is a Bangladesh-based ready-made garment (RMG) sourcing house. We don't run our own factory floor — instead, we operate a carefully vetted network of production partners across Dhaka, Gazipur, Chattogram and Narayanganj, and manage every order end-to-end on behalf of our clients.

That structure means clients get the pricing and flexibility of direct factory sourcing, paired with the accountability of a dedicated merchandising and quality team standing behind every order.

Why It Works

Mission, vision, and how we operate

Our Mission

Make sourcing from Bangladesh predictable — matching every client to the right factory partner and standing behind quality and timelines at every stage.

Our Vision

To be the most transparent sourcing partner working out of Bangladesh — where clients always know exactly where their order stands.

How We Work

Dedicated merchandisers, on-ground QC staff, and weekly reporting — so a factory 8,000 miles away feels like it's down the hall.

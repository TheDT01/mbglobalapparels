What We Do

End-to-end sourcing, handled for you

From first sample to final shipment, we manage every step of production so you don't have to manage a factory from another continent.

01 — Design

Product Development & Sampling

Tech pack review, fit and proto samples, and rapid revision cycles before bulk commitment.

02 — Sourcing

Fabric & Trim Sourcing

Vetted mills across knit and woven bases, matched to your quality tier and price target.

03 — Production

Factory & Vendor Management

Allocation to the right partner factory, capacity booking, and on-ground coordination.

04 — Quality

Quality Assurance & Compliance

In-line and final inspections, AQL reporting, and social/technical compliance checks.

05 — Tracking

Production Monitoring

Weekly WIP reports so you always know exactly where an order stands on the floor.

06 — Delivery

Logistics & Shipping

Documentation, freight-forwarder coordination, and on-time delivery to your DC.
